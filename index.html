Salt and Sanctuary Cheat Sheet

The following is a checklist and set of information for use when playing Salt and Sanctuary. If this is your first time visiting this page please check out the Help section. This checklist was created by adopting the source code from Zachary Kjellberg's Dark Soul Cheat Sheet. The walkthrough is thanks to the Fextralife wiki.

Warning: Contains Spoilers!

Playthrough Checklist

  • Shivering Shore
  • The Festering Banquet
  • Village of Smiles
  • The Watching Woods
  • Sunken Keep
  • Cemetery of Ash

Shivering Shore

  • Talk to the Old Man and join The Three, The Iron Ones, or Devara's Light.
  • Proceed forward and pick up the Sanctuary Key from the chest on the path.
  • Claim the first sanctuary!
  • Leave the sanctuary to the right, jump onto the roof, and open the chest for 3x Red Shards and a Pouch of Salt

The Festering Banquet

  • Enter the castle, head up and out the first door to the left for a Stone Merchant and a Bundle of Salt.
  • Kick the ladder down to unlock a shortcut to this level.
  • Head up and to the right and pick up a Bell of Return near a crossbowman and a feral beast.
  • Continue upward to the right, heading left when necessary to climb, until you arrive at this area's shrine.
  • Exit up and to the left, ascend the ladder, and slay the Sodden Knight for the Fortress Key.
  • Head down from the shrine and activate the level to unlock a shortcut back toward the Shivering Shore.
  • Open the locked door to your right using the Fortress Key.
  • Speak to the Masterless Knight.
  • Drop down the soft ledge and head left back into the banquet. Climb up the steps and unlock another shortcut back to the earlier parts of the banquet.
  • Head down the large set of stairs and pick up a Torch, Bundle of Salt, and a Soldier's Spear.
  • Continue left across the crumbling platforms and open a shortcut back to the Shivering Shore sanctuary.
  • Head down the first crumbling platform from the shortcut and head into the Village of Smiles.

Village of Smiles

  • Head left, past the feral beasts and bronze knight, to a structure with several drowned enemies. Pick up the Pouch of Salt and three Lock of Hair.
  • Continue left and talk to the Scarecrow.
  • Continue left, down the ledges at the edge of the cliff to find a sanctuary to The Three with a blacksmith.
  • Head back to the Scarecrow and climb the structure to enter a cave.
  • Descend to the lower floor, kill the bronze knight, and open a hidden door to the right to unlock a room with a Wrapped Link.
  • Continue heading down and kill the Queen of Smiles.
  • Open the chest to the right for the chef's set: an Iron Pot, Chef's Trousers, Chef's Apron, and Chef's Toque.
  • Turn left immediately on the stairs to open a hidden door. Descend down the hanging cliffs for a Bloodlower Charm.
  • Head right from the chest and up a ladder and pull the lever to unlock a shortcut to the Village of Smiles Sanctuary.
  • Climb the hanging cliffs above the cave entrance and jump into the partially-obscured room to find a Kureimoa.
  • Head back into the cave to enter The Watching Woods.

The Watching Woods

  • Head right and down to a candle-lit platform with a Pouch of Salt and 3x Antidote.
  • Head up and to the right to a stone platform with items in the two corners. The left has 3x Antidote and the right has a Bullwhip and 3x Antidote.
  • Continue on and pick up the Stone Sellsword and a Buckler on a stone platform.
  • Take the ladder down to find a sanctuary dedicated to The Stone Roots.
  • Head back up and up the stairs to defeat The Mad Alchemist.
  • Pick up the Mossy Key and unlock the door to the right of the sanctuary.
  • Grab A Soldier's Poem and a Stone Blacksmith on a ledge in the cave.
  • Optionally, take the elevator on the left down to the Kraekan Cyclops.
  • Head right from the elevator to find 3x Bell of Return near a broken bridge and a Warhammer after a cave.
  • Head right to the Sunken Keep.

Sunken Keep

  • Head up a long ladder and through a cave to find an unclaimed sanctuary.
  • Open a hidden door up a set of stairs to the left of the sanctuary for a Stone Merchant and a Pack of Salt.
  • Climb upwards and dispatch of The False Jester.

Cemetery of Ash

  • From the start head right at the first turn and pick up the Soul of a Deserted Corpse
  • Loot the corpse on the fountain to obtain the Ashen Estus Flask
  • Turn right after the fountain and head down the path through a flooded passage. Kill the Ravenous Crystal Lizard to get a Titanite Scale and then pick up the Soul of an Unknown Traveler from the corpse in the area
  • Head back to the fountain and take the path straight ahead. Climb the cliff and rest at the Cemetery of Ash bonfire to receive the Rest gesture
  • From the bonfire head down the left path and jump from the cliff over to the corpse on the tomb to pick up a Titanite Shard
  • Take the side ridge down from the large cliff face on the right to pick up 5 Firebombs from a nearby corpse
  • Back on the main path head through the archway and take the sword embedded in the statue. Kill Iudex Gundyr to receive the Coiled Sword
  • Congratulate yourself on beating the first boss of Dark Souls 3 and rest at the Iudex Gundyr bonfire
  • Continue through the large door and pick up the Broken Straight Sword from the corpse leaning on the tombstone
  • Head over to the large cliff edge and pick up the Homeward Bone at the edge of the ridge
  • Take the left path up the cliff and turn left just before Firelink Shrine and grab the East-West Shield from the corpse on the tree
  • Kill Sword Master Saber to get the Uchigatana, Master's Attire, and Master's Gloves, and to unlock his summon sign
  • Head back and up the path to the left to pick up an Ember from a corpse above the entrance above Firelink Shrine
  • Continue across to the other side picking up a Homeward Bone from a corpse behind a tomb and another Ember from the lower ridge
  • Go back to the main path and enter Firelink Shrine

Help

Can I use this for multiple characters?

Yup, use the profile selector and buttons at the top right of the page to setup multiple characters.

How do I help with feedback, corrections, suggestions or additions?

You can visit the GitHub repository and report Issues or create a fork and submit a Pull Request.

How does the checklist status get saved?

It gets saved to the local storage in your browser and not to any database or API. So if you are clearing your browser cache make sure not to delete the site data or your checklists will get reset.
